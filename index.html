About Me

Technical Curiosity Meets Creative Energy

My background spans IT, data science, digital problem-solving, and creative work. I love exploring how systems work beneath the surface — whether that means debugging code, training machine learning models, organizing messy datasets, or designing interfaces that feel polished and intuitive.
