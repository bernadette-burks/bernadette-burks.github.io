Projects

This project showcases my data refining skills as they’ve grown over time. I revisited a project from my early days at New England College titled Trends in Opioid Misuse and Abuse in the U.S. (2016–2017). With clinical insight and deeper technical skill, I transformed it using more efficient dplyr pipelines, refined visualizations, and clearer narrative flow.

This is more than a portfolio piece — it’s a personal evolution in clarity, courage, and refinement.

In this ongoing series, I explore how visual storytelling can reveal patterns otherwise buried in data. These projects include:
  • 💡 A seasonal trends dashboard using ggplot2 and plotly
  • 🎭 A mental health insight report combining clinical intuition with clean, empathetic design
  • 🌍 A geospatial case study visualizing opioid impact by state using leaflet

Each visualization is crafted to speak clearly and respectfully — not just to numbers, but to the stories they hold.
