Projects

This project showcases my data refining skills as they have grown over time. I decided to fine-tune a data project I had completed earlier in my studies at New England College that aligns with my clinical background - Trends in Opioid Misuse and Abuse in the United States Between 2016 and 2017. There is a "student version" and a "professional version". I'm mainly looking for ways to remove redundancy and fine-tune the dyplyr package features for cleaner code and more accessible visualizations. In sharing both my "then" and "now" versions, I hope to reveal something no résumé bullet ever could: the courage to share my own mistakes while revamping and refining them with elegance!
